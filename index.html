In Honoured Memory

This memorial book is dedicated to the recruits of the Depot Nigerian Army who paid the ultimate price — brave young men and women who answered the call to serve their nation and gave their lives in the course of training and duty.

Their sacrifice shall not be forgotten. Nigeria honours the memory of each hero enshrined in these pages.

“The greatest tribute we can offer is to remember them, honour their memory, and continue the mission they gave their lives for.”
